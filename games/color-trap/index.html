    Color Trap

    How to play

    Each round draws one trap. Red and Blue take turns placing one piece on any empty space.

    The player whose move completes the trap in their own color loses the round. Rotations and mirror images count exactly as shown; empty spaces inside a pattern stay empty spacing, not required empty cells.

    The first player to win three rounds wins the match. The starting color alternates each round.
